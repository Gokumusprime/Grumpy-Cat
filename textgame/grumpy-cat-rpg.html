You wake up one morning and realize that you have assumed the form of Grumpy Cat, one of the worlds most loved/hated creatures. Before you are given a chance to figure what is going on, a giant rat appears and declares that you have stolen his cheese. You don't remember ever taking this rat's cheese and try to explain this to him. The rat does not seem to care and atempts to use tackle on you. Engage the enemy!
